Planning & Content Collection

User Analysis & Content Creation/Collection

I use a simple, but effective User Group Analysis process to identify and understand who your clients are. Ideally, we'll break them out into two or three key demographics in order to identify;

  • Why they would go to your site
  • What information they want from your site
  • What actions you want them to take

Using this information, we'll plan out each page of your website by drawing simple "wire frames" on paper or a whiteboard. These wire frames will describe the content layout and organization that best meet the needs of your clients and achieve your business goals.
